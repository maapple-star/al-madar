Methodology

Five phases, one programme spine.

Every engagement passes through the same five gates. Each gate has a documented owner, a deliverable, and a sign-off cadence — no surprises at handover.

Phase01

Discovery

Site walks, stakeholder interviews, programme constraints, and risk surfacing. We deliver a written brief response within 14 days of first engagement.

Cycle2 weeks
Phase02

Design Coordination

Architect-led; we coordinate trades, MEP clash detection, and constructability review. LOD 400 BIM signed off before pre-con.

Cycle6—10 weeks
Phase03

Pre-Construction

Procurement, long-lead orders, mock-rooms, sample boards, and trade onboarding. Programme baselined and locked.

Cycle4—8 weeks
Phase04

Delivery

Site execution under a single PM. Daily look-aheads, weekly client reports, monthly executive reviews. Quality gate at every trade transition.

Cycleper programme
Phase05

Handover & Aftercare

Snag-free handover, full O&M documentation, training, and 24-month aftercare with on-call response. Annual condition reports for hospitality clients.

Aftercare24 months

About the house

A house, not a holding.

Al Madar MGM Construction L.L.C. has been privately held and quietly operated since 1979. We build for clients who care about the receipts: museum boards, hospitality groups, civic authorities, and private principals across the GCC.

The studio is led by a third-generation operator and a senior PM cadre averaging 22 years at the house. We turn down work that doesn't suit us. We hand over what we sign for. The signature stays on every door we build.

We are not a holding, we are not a roll-up, we are not chasing IPO. We're a construction house — one signature, one set of standards, four decades of file references on request.
