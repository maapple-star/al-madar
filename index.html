Twenty homepage directions —
one shared content model.

A side-by-side study of how the same firm — Al Madar MGM Construction L.L.C, established 1979 — could read across twenty distinct aesthetic positions. v1 — v5 are design-led. v6 — v10 target enterprise procurement, sovereign clients and capital-markets stakeholders. v11 — v15 are modern and sleek — Apple, Linear, Stripe, Swiss precision and kinetic editorial. v16 — v20 are refined enterprise — spatial glass, live operations, institutional whitepaper, long-form essay and performance luxury. Same hero copy, nine disciplines, the same projects, four pillars, six certifications. Only the visual treatment changes.
