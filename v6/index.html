— III. Capabilities

Nine disciplines.
One responsible contractor.

Each capability tracks against environmental, social and governance metrics — disclosed annually in the Al Madar sustainability report.

  1. 01 Civil construction Low and mid-rise structures, refurbishments, additions. RCC frames, ground works, MEP, completion. → EPD concrete · 62%
  2. 02 Fit-out Commercial, hospitality, retail, residential. End-to-end interior delivery. → FSC timber · 84%
  3. 03 Maintenance Annual maintenance contracts, facilities, planned upkeep. Many clients have stayed twenty years. → Lifecycle audits
  4. 04 Specialist finishes Venetian plaster, micro-cement, tadelakt, marmorino. Hand-applied, hand-burnished. → Lime-based · low-VOC
  5. 05 Decorative arts Murals, gilding, hand-painted ornament. 22-carat gold leaf, palladium. → In-house atelier
  6. 06 Architectural concrete Polished, board-formed, exposed-aggregate. Cast-in-place finishes. → Low-carbon mix
  7. 07 Decorative flooring Epoxy, terrazzo, resin, micro-screed. → Recycled aggregate
  8. 08 Metal coatings Architectural metallics, patinas, brass & copper finishes. → Solvent-free
  9. 09 Theming Themed environments — sculpted GRC, faux finishes, set design crossed with permanent construction. → GRC · low embodied
