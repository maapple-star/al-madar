— 01 / About// origins

Forty-seven years of receipts.
Now plugged in.

A construction house combining the engineering discipline of a contractor with the craftsmanship of a finishes atelier — delivered through a digital-first project stack.

A privately held UAE construction house. Forty-seven years building this country — from structure to surface, plus telemetry.

Al Madar MGM was established in 1979, predating modern Dubai's construction boom by a decade. We deliver across nine in-house disciplines — civil construction, fit-out, maintenance, specialist finishes, decorative arts, architectural concrete, decorative flooring, metal coatings and theming.

Today, every active site is wired. BIM 360 for federated models, Procore for project ops, Bentley iTwin for digital twins, and a custom site-IoT layer that streams progress, environment and quality telemetry to a single dashboard the client can log into at any time.

Most peers either deliver craftsmanship or run modern tooling. We do both — without compromise on either. Our oldest active maintenance contract dates to 1991; our newest active digital twin is six weeks old.
