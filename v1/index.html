— II. The studio

A construction house run like an atelier.

Most contractors stop where the paint begins. We don't. Al Madar MGM was founded in Dubai in 1979 to build the new city — and stayed long enough to learn its oldest crafts. Today the studio combines a working civil-construction division with a specialist atelier for plasters, gilding, murals and theming.

The model is unusual on purpose. A single trusted partner takes a project from foundation to final coat, with the same standards applied to a structural slab and to a hand-burnished tadelakt wall. Procurement teams describe it as the rare case where the contractor and the craftsman are the same firm.

Forty-seven years in, we are still a privately held UAE house — small enough to know every wall on every site, large enough to carry hospitality, cultural and government mandates at scale.

M. Mansoor Al Madar  ·  Founding Partner  ·  Est. 1979
